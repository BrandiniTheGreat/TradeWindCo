Fleet cycle glut.

The big charter operators — Moorings, Sunsail, Dream Yacht — run 5-year fleet cycles. Every year they buy hundreds of new catamarans and dump the predecessor inventory. Moorings alone took 160 new hulls in 2024. The supply is durable and predictable.

2025 US tariff jump on foreign-built cats

Frozen US buyer pool.

The natural buyer is American. But 2025 tariffs took the import duty from 1.5% to 20-25% — overnight. Add customs paperwork, ocean delivery, USCG documentation, and the on-island survey coordination, and most DIY buyers walk. The friction is the moat.

$2-3K/mo
Per-boat dockage on unsold ex-fleet inventory

Motivated sellers.

Fleet operators pay ongoing slip fees, insurance, and maintenance on every unsold boat. Each cut they take comes off our cost basis. Ex-fleet boats that retail for $550K-$600K in the US sit at $275K-$400K in BVI brokerage and the asks keep coming down.
