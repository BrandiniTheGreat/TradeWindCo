Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-18

Trade Wind & Co. ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you interact with our website (), how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Information you give us directly — when you submit an inquiry, request an inspection, or contact us by phone or email. This typically includes your name, phone number, email address, budget range, timeline, destination port, and any free-text notes you choose to share.
  • Information collected automatically — when you visit our site, our hosting provider and analytics service may log standard request data: your IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, referring URL, and approximate geographic region. We use this only in aggregate to improve the site.
  • Cookies and local storage — we use browser local storage to remember your saved-favorites watchlist (if you opt to use it). We do not currently set tracking cookies for advertising purposes. If we add analytics that uses cookies in the future, we will update this policy and add appropriate consent mechanisms.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about catamarans, pricing, inspections, and the import process
  • Coordinate inspections, surveys, and other steps in a purchase transaction
  • Send you transactional emails relating to a deal you are actively in
  • Send you market commentary or new-listing updates, only if you have opted in
  • Improve the website and our services
  • Comply with legal obligations, including those imposed under the Florida Yacht and Ship Brokers Act (FS Chapter 326)

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with parties necessary to perform the services you have engaged us for, including:

  • Source brokers in the BVI, Bahamas, and Caribbean (e.g., Moorings Brokerage, BVI Yacht Sales, The Multihull Company, Just Catamarans) when coordinating an inspection or acquisition on your behalf
  • Marine surveyors when scheduling an inspection
  • Maritime attorneys in the US and BVI for purchase contract and title work
  • Maritime escrow agents (e.g., Yachtworld Escrow) for funds handling
  • Customs brokers, delivery captains, and USCG documentation services as required to complete an import
  • Service providers that operate our website and infrastructure (e.g., our hosting provider Vercel; our form-submission processor Formspree; our email provider Google Workspace)
  • Government authorities when legally required (e.g., US Customs and Border Protection, the US Coast Guard, the Florida Department of Business and Professional Regulation)

4. Data retention

We retain inquiry and transaction records as long as necessary to provide services and to comply with our legal obligations, typically seven (7) years after the last interaction. You may request deletion of your information at any time, subject to record-keeping requirements imposed by Florida law on regulated yacht brokers and dealers.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion (subject to our retention obligations under FS 326)
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information (we do not sell or share for cross-context behavioral advertising)

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Residents of the European Economic Area, UK, and other jurisdictions have rights under their applicable privacy laws. To exercise any right, email us at .

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information you provide. Form submissions are transmitted over HTTPS. Our hosting and form-processing infrastructure providers are SOC 2 / ISO 27001 audited. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure, however, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

Our services are intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be flagged on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
